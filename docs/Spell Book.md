## Defense Spells

### Sleep Monster

* Spell Level: 3
* Base mana: 4
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* causes the targeted monster to fall into an light, enchanted sleep. Any attempt to attack the monster while it sleeps will awaken it

### Slow Monster

* Spell Level: 3
* Base mana: 4
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect: causes the targeted monster to move at about half its normal speed. Note that the effects of several of these spells can accumulate, but each successive one is less effective than the last. This means a monster will move at 1/2, then 1/3, then 1/4, then 1/5 ... of its normal speed.  The effect wears off in 10 in-game minutes.

### Heal Minor Wounds

* Spell level: 3

### Healing

* Spell level: 5
* Base mana: 6

### Neutralize Poison

* Spell level: 2

## Movement Spells

### Phase Door

* Spell level: 1
* Base mana: 1
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect: Transports a player to a random location from 5 to 10 squares (inclusive) from his or her current position.  The player will be on the same level.

### Teleport

* Spell level: 3
* Base mana: 3
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect: Transports a player to a random tile on the same level that is *at least* 10 squares from his or her current location. 

### Levitation

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 2
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect: Allows the player to walk on air for a short time, avoiding many gravity traps.  This includes all known traps except Glyphs of Warding. 

## Rune of Return

* Spell level: 3
* Base mana: 3
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect:
  * From inside a dungeon, the spell will return the player to the ground level, bypassing the levels in between. 
  * From ground level, the spell teleports the player to the deepest place the player has visited so far.
  * It is possible to die during the 5 second window. 

## Divination Spells

### Detect Objects

* Spell Level: 1
* Base mana: 1
* Game clock: 30 seconds
* effect:
  * if the spell is interrupted in the 30 second window, ie if the player is attacked before the 30 seconds in-game clock finishes, there is no effect.
  * Shows the location of all the objects on the current level. 
  * These sites will remain seen, but if the objects are later moved by some creature, this will not be discovered until the site is actually visited.

### Detect Monsters

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 2
* Game clock: 30 seconds
* effect:
  * if the spell is interrupted in the 30 second window, ie if the player is attacked before the 30 seconds in-game clock finishes, there is no effect.
  * Shows the location of all monsters on the current level at the time of casting.
  * Lasts for 30 minutes of in-game time, plus 1 minute for each level of the character casting the spell. 
  * Carries from level to level if the player changes level. 

### Detect Traps

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 2
* Game clock: 30 seconds
* effect: 
  * if the spell is interrupted in the 30 second window, ie if the player is attacked before the 30 seconds in-game clock finishes, there is no effect.
  * Traps within 10 squares of the player are guaranteed to be found.
  * Traps previously discovered have a reduced probability of being triggered. 
  * Probability of trap detection decreases with distance at an unknown rate. 
  * Only effects the current level

### Identify

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 2
* Game clock: 60 seconds
* effect:
  * Only effects a single, selected, unidentified object within your pack, belt, or on the floor in the same tile as the player. 
  * If no unidentified object is present, the spell has no effect, but mana is still used. 
  * The player is presented a menu of eligible objects from which to select one.
  * The selected object is identified, including type of object, any curse or enchantment, and if applicable, number of charges (which may be zero).  Number of charges is only applicable for wands and staffs. 
  * If the object is enchanted or cursed, the enchantment or curse does *not* affect the player until the object is placed in the corresponding slot (ie a helmet in the helmet slot). 

### Light

* Spell level: 1
* Base mana: 1
* Game clock: 5 seconds
* effect:
  * Cast in a hallway, it will light the 3x3 square region around the target square.
  * Cast in a room, the entire room is lit.
  * Cast on the surface one of the surface maps including the village, either town, or the area between village and mine, between town and fortress, or between town and castle) will have no effect but mana will be used. 


### Clairvoyance

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 3
* Game clock: 30 seconds
* effect:
  * if the spell is interrupted in the 30 second window, ie if the player is attacked before the 30 seconds in-game clock finishes, there is no effect.
  * The player is asked to select a target. The target may be any location on the current level.  a square 10x10x showing all secret doors and traps surrounding that target is revealed. 
  * Cast on the surface one of the surface maps including the village, either town, or the area between village and mine, between town and fortress, or between town and castle) will have no effect but mana will be used.

## Miscellaneous Spells

### Remove Curse

* Spell level: 2
* Base mana: 2
* Game clock: 60 seconds
* effect:
  * if the spell is interrupted in the 60 second window, ie if the player is attacked before the 60 seconds in-game clock finishes, there is no effect.
  * Removes the curse from items with a negative bonus. 
  * Allows such items to be removed from the corresponding slot (ie a helmet from the helmet slot). 
  * Merchants will *probably* still refuse to purchase such items. 
  * Same restrictions as the Identify spell, except it also applies to items being worn in the correct slot. 

### Create Traps

* effect:
